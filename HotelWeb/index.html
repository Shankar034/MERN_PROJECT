OUR HOTEL

Hotel Siddhartha is a luxurious 4 star hotel in the bustling town of Nepalgunj. Situated between Ranjha Airport (3 Km) and Nepalgunj town, the Hotel offers a vast range of facilities such as fitness center, swimming pool, health club, badminton court etc. The Hotel is an ideal venue for workshop, seminar, party as it boasts conference rooms, banquet hall, swimming pool and ample parking space.
